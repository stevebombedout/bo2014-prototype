About us

Bombed Out Records is a non-profit, do-it-yourself record label based in Leeds and Harrogate in the UK. Our first record was released in 1998.

We’ve released records by: The Amistad, Blocko, The Brown Hound James Band, The Dauntless Elite, Dropnose, Dugong, Eighty Six, Fig. 4.0, Homebrew, Jets Vs Sharks, Joe Ninety, Kelly 8, The Leif Ericsson, The Mercy Suite, Peachfuzz, Remainderfour, Saturday’s Kids, The Take, Wooderson and Zapiain.

Please get in touch for trades/wholesale enquiries.
